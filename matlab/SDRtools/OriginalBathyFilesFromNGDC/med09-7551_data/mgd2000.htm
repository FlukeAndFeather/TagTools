NGDC Marine Geophysical Data Formats MGD-2000

MGD-2000

MARINE GEOPHYSICAL DATA EXCHANGE FORMATS

NATIONAL GEOPHYSICAL DATA CENTER
NATIONAL OCEANIC AND ATMOSPHERIC ADMINISTRATION
NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL SATELLITE, DATA, AND INFORMATION SERVICE
U.S. DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE

The National Geophysical Data Center (NGDC) has developed a series of data formats for various purposes. Among these are several formats for the exchange, storage and dissemination of marine geophysical data. These MGD-2000 formats are, as the name implies Year 2000 Compliant, and are used extensively by the GEODAS software. The Formats Descriptions:
  • MARINE GEOPHYSICAL DATA EXCHANGE FORMAT - MGD77
  • AROMAGNETIC SURVEYS HEADER FORMAT - ARO88
  • HYDROGRAPHIC SURVEYS DATA EXCHANGE FORMAT - HYD93
  • GEODAS Gridded Data FORMAT - GRD98
  • GEODAS 2-D Vector FORMAT - VCT00
  • GEODAS BINARY INTEGER XYZ FORMAT - XYZ03
